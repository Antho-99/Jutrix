Who are we?

We are a company which has huge ambitions in terms of medecine. Our aim is to revolutionize it by offering electrode-based technology.
Our Headquarters are located in New York, USA.

What do we propose?

We want to give people with reduced mobility a better life, by providing them with clothes made of electrodes. They would send electrical impulses to their bodies, allowing them to move their limbs easier.
All of this will be worked on in our research center.
